About

Welcome to Evergreen Coffee Co

At Evergreen Coffee Co, we blend a passion for quality with a love for local culture. Our expert baristas craft each cup using the finest beans, from rich espressos to smooth pour-overs and comforting lattes.

But it’s not just about the coffee. Our cozy space, friendly staff, and curated pastries create the perfect spot to start your day, take a break, or connect with friends. We’re dedicated to making everyone feel at home.

Come by Evergreen Coffee Co and enjoy a place where every visit feels like coming home. We can’t wait to share our love for coffee and community with you!

Nova Lox
Bagel

A delicious Nova lox bagel is a culinary delight that features a perfectly toasted bagel topped with silky, thinly sliced Nova lox. This premium smoked salmon is paired with creamy cream cheese, and often enhanced with crisp red onions, capers, and fresh dill.

Happy Customer

A great selection of coffee, breakfast and lunch food options. I ordered a latte with an extra slot of expresso and it was perfect. I also ordered a donut, a croissant, a breakfast sandwich and a pastrami sandwich and all got good reviews from the family. The coffee shop is right in front of a nice plaza with a water fixture with benches. A nice place to sit and enjoy your coffee.

Dani V.

San Francisco, CA

Great local shop. We went here after going to the Farmers Market across the street last Sunday. Wish we had gone in before!

Went in for a hot coffee and a latte, both were good. Then we decided to try one of their sandwiches. Gotta say it was a very good roast beef sandwich.

Customer service was very good, everyone is so friendly and helpful. We'll definitely be making this a regular stop. There are a few tables inside along with two counters with bar stools. There's also a covered area outside with several tables and chairs which will be great on the warm summer days.
