About

Welcome to Evergreen Coffee Co

At Evergreen Coffee Co, we blend a passion for quality with a love for local culture. Our expert baristas craft each cup using the finest beans, from rich espressos to smooth pour-overs and comforting lattes.

But it’s not just about the coffee. Our cozy space, friendly staff, and curated pastries create the perfect spot to start your day, take a break, or connect with friends. We’re dedicated to making everyone feel at home.

Come by Evergreen Coffee Co and enjoy a place where every visit feels like coming home. We can’t wait to share our love for coffee and community with you!

Evergreen Coffee Co

Explore Evergreen Coffee Co and Its Community
Evergreen Coffee Co in San Jose is more than just a coffee shop; it’s a local hub of activity.

Farmer’s Markets: Weekly Freshness
Visit the Evergreen Farmer’s Market every Wednesday and Sunday morning, just a short walk from the café. Discover local produce, beautiful flowers and enjoy a lively market atmosphere.

Charming Plaza: Perfect for Relaxing
The beautiful plaza in front of Evergreen Coffee Co is an ideal spot to savor your coffee. With outdoor seating and scenic views, it’s perfect for catching up with friends or enjoying a quiet moment.

Library Events: Engage Locally
Nearby, the local library hosts engaging events like book readings and workshops. Afterward, stop by Evergreen Coffee Co to discuss your finds or just unwind with a coffee.
